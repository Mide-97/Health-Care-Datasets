# IOTB TECH Hospital data analysis

---
After analysing the healthcare datasets that was provided by IOTB TECH Hospital,the datasets were summarized using pivot tables,pivot charts and data analysis techniquues in excel.

---
## Patient Analysis
- The total number of patients admitted is 54,966.
- From the 54,966 patients that was admitted,27,496 patients were Male while the remaining 27,470 patients were Female.
- The most common blood group from the admitted patients is A- blood type
- The highest admission falls between age group 53-62 which has the number of patients admitted to be 8,207. ![alt text](<images/Admission by age group.png>)

---
## Medical Condition Analysis
- The most common medical condition is Arthritis.![alt text](<images/Most Common Medical Condition.png>)
- The medical condition that generates the highest revenue is Diabetes which has the total revenue of #236,486,971
- The medical condition that keeps patients in the hospital the longest is Asthma

---
## Revenue Analysis
- The total hospital revenue is ₦1,404,068,339
- The revenue is fluctuating with a bit increase in August but later declined back in September.
![alt text](images/Monthly%20Revenue%20Trend.png)
- The insurance provider with the highest billing amount is Cigna insurance provider with total billing amount of #284,334,099.
![alt text](<images/Insurance Provider Versus Billing Amount.png>)
- Elective admission generates the highest revenue.![alt text](<images/Admission Type Versus Revenue.png>)

---
## Admission Analysis
- The most common admision type is Elective admission type
- The highest admission of patients occur in the month of August which also has the highest revenue
- The female gender has the highest emergency admission.
![alt text](<images/Gender With The Highest Emergency Admission.png>)
